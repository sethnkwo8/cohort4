Casemiro Unveils New number...

We can confirm that Casemiro will wear the no.18 shirt for Manchester United,
following the completion of his transfer from Real Madrid. The Brazilian's jersey
is now available to order via United Direct. Casemiro's arrival was confirmed on
Monday night and he was swiftly unveiled in front of a raucous crowd at Old Trafford
ahead of the 2-1 Premier League win over Liverpool. On Thursday, the five-time
Champions League winner trained with his new team-mates for the first time and he is
notably available for Saturday’s match against Southampton at St Mary’s Stadium.
